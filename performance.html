Top Performance Cars

Toyota Supra

The Toyota Supra is an iconic sports car known for its aggressive styling and high-performance engineering. Powered by a **3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine**, it produces **382 horsepower** and **500 Nm of torque**. With a **top speed of 250 km/h**, the Supra delivers breathtaking acceleration and precise handling. In India, it comes at an approximate price of **?85 lakh**.

Pagani Huayra

The Pagani Huayra is a masterpiece of Italian craftsmanship, featuring a **6.0L twin-turbocharged V12** engine from AMG. It delivers an astounding **720 horsepower** and **1000 Nm of torque**, achieving a **top speed of 383 km/h**. The Huayra is a limited-production hypercar with an exquisite carbon-fiber body, priced at around **?23 crore** in India.

Koenigsegg Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko is a marvel of speed and aerodynamics, featuring a **5.0L twin-turbocharged V8** engine. Producing up to **1600 horsepower** on E85 fuel, it is one of the most powerful hypercars ever made. With a projected **top speed of 531 km/h**, the Jesko is built for extreme performance. This beast costs around **?28 crore** in India.

BMW M5 Competition

The BMW M5 Competition is a high-performance luxury sedan with a **4.4L twin-turbo V8**. It delivers **625 horsepower** and **750 Nm of torque**, sprinting from **0-100 km/h in just 3.3 seconds**. With a **top speed of 305 km/h**, it balances power and comfort effortlessly. In India, it is priced at **?1.8 crore**.

Nissan GT-R

The Nissan GT-R, famously known as "Godzilla," features a **3.8L twin-turbo V6** engine. Producing **565 horsepower** and **633 Nm of torque**, it reaches a **top speed of 315 km/h**. The GT-R's legendary all-wheel-drive system ensures unmatched grip and acceleration. In India, it costs approximately **?2.1 crore**.

Porsche 911 Turbo S

The Porsche 911 Turbo S is a perfect blend of luxury and performance, featuring a **3.7L twin-turbo flat-6**. It generates **640 horsepower** and **800 Nm of torque**, reaching **0-100 km/h in just 2.6 seconds**. With a **top speed of 330 km/h**, it is one of the fastest sports cars available. In India, it is priced at **?3.5 crore**.
